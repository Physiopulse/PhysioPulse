About Us

Dr. S. RAJA DURAI, BPT

Founder & Editor-in-Chief

PhysioPulse was founded to create a dedicated academic journal for physiotherapy. The platform focuses on rehabilitation science, neuro-musculoskeletal physiotherapy, therapeutic exercise, and prevention.We believe in understanding the science behind movement and applying it with clarity, ethics, and purpose. Through learning, writing, and practical training, We are preparing ourself to become a competent and compassionate physiotherapist committed to improving functional outcomes and quality of life.
